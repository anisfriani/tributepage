Andrea Hirata

"Bermimpilah, maka Tuhan akan mememluk mimpi-mimpimu." Andrea Hirata, Sang Pemimpi

Andrea Hirata was born on October 24, 1967 in Gantung, Belitong is an Indonesian author. While he was young, his parents changed his name seven times. They settled on the name Andrea while the name Hirata was given by his mother. He grew up in a poor family not far from a goverment-owned mine

Hirata started his tertiary education with a degree in economics from The University of Indonesia. After receiving a scholarship from the European Union, he didi his master's degree in Europe. First at the University of Paris then at Sheffield Hallam University, Britaon.

Hirata released Laskar Pelangi in 2005. Its also spawned three sequel: Sang Pemimpi, Edensor and Maryamah Karpov.

Awards

  • Winner of New York Book Festival 2013 in General Category
  • Granted an Honorary Doctor of Letter from University of Warwick, UK in 2015
